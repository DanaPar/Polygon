How to use the Tool
Follow these steps to convert your polygon data.
Google Earth to LGS
  1. Export your polygon from Google Earth as a .kml file.
  2. Drag and drop that file into the dashed box or click to select it.
  3. Press the Google Earth to LGS button.
  4. Copy the generated coordinates from the text area.
LGS to Google Earth
  1. Paste your LGS coordinate string into the text box.
  2. Click LGS to Google Earth.
  3. A .kml file will be generated for you to download and open in Google Earth.
